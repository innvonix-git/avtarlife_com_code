About us

AvatarLife is a blockchain enabled virtual reality platform. The currency used is AVRA a Greek synonym of the English word “AURA”. (The aura is a non-physical field of energies around a person, visible through clairvoyance.) AVRA, derived from the neo blockchain, enables AvatarLife to be more robust and efficient than the competing Virtual reality worlds who decided to use the Ethereum blockchain.

AvatarLife aims to achieve breakthroughs in e-commerce and online paid gaming by enabling high quality virtual reality based experiences. AvatarLife also looks to cater to the masses through optimized programming to allow the software work on low to mid end computers for non virtual reality experience.
